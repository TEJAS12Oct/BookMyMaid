DATABASE TABLES INFORMATION
NAME , PRIMARY & FORIEGN KEY 
  
1. MAID 
   ID AUTO_INCREMENT (PRIMARY KEY) , NAME , USERNAME , PASSWORD , GENDER , AGE , 
   MOBILE_NO , EMAIL_ID , ADDRESS , CITY , PINCODE , ADHAR_CARD , 
   EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_ROOM , EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_MEMBER
   POLICE_VERIFICATION_CERTIFICATE , EXPERIENCE , IMAGES  

2. USER 
   ID AUTO_INCREMENT (PRIMARY KEY) , NAME , USERNAME , PASSWORD, 
   GENDER , FAMILY_MEMBERS , ROOMS , MOBILE_NO , EMAIL_ID, 
   ADDRESS , CITY , PINCODE , ADHAR_CARD , IMAGES 
   
3. SERVICES 
   ID AUTO_INCREMENT (PRIMARY KEY) , SERVICE , BASE_CHARGES 

4. BOOKING_INFO
   ID AUTO_INCREMENT (PRIMARY KEY) , 
   MAID_ID REFERENCE (MAID INFO TABLE) ID (FORIEGN KEY) , 
   USER_ID REFERENCE (USER INFO TABLE) ID (FORIEGN KEY) , 
   SERVICE_ID REFERENCE (SERVICES TABLE) ID (FORIEGN KEY) ,
   MAID_EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_ROOM (MAID INFO TABLE) EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_ROOM (FORIEGN KEY) , 
   MAID_EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_MEMBER (MAID INFO TABLE) EXTRA_CHARGE_PER_MEMBER (FORIEGN KEY),
   USER_FAMILY_MEMBERS (MAID INFO TABLE) FAMILY_MEMBERS (FORIEGN KEY), 
   USER_ROOMS (MAID INFO TABLE) ROOMS (FORIEGN KEY),
   BOOKING_DATE ,
   MONTH_CHARGES , 
   TIME_SLOTS ,
   HOLIDAYS  

5. MAID_REVIEW 
   ID AUTO_INCREMENT (PRIMARY KEY) , 
   MAID_ID REFERENCE (MAID INFO TABLE) ID (FORIEGN KEY),  
   USER_ID REFERENCE (USER INFO TABLE) ID (FORIEGN KEY) ,
   RATING  , 
   COMMENTS
